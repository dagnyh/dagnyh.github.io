Exercise

Get Moving, Be Healthy

Exercise is vital for your health. Make sure you're staying active and you'll have more energy and feel better overall.

Exercise Intensity Levels

Level of Intensity Perceived Exertion Physical Cues
Light Easy Does not induce sweat unless it's hot and humid. No noticeable change in breathing patterns.
Moderate Somewhat hard Induces sweat after 10 minutes. Breathing becomes deeper and faster. Conversation possible, but not singing.
High Hard Induces sweat after 3-5 minutes. Breathing is deep and rapid. Speech possbile only in short phrases.

Types of Exercise

Don't let the word "exercise" scare you! There are many different ways to stay active, including:

  • Yoga
  • Swimming
  • Walking
  • Biking

Vary Your Workouts

It's easy to get overwhelmed when beginning to exercise. Luckily, you don't have to work out every muscle in one day! Separate muscle groups:

  • Triceps/chest
  • Biceps/back
  • Abs/shoulders
  • Legs

30 Minutes a Day

Make sure to get at least 30 minutes of exercise every day. Tips:

  • Don't get frustrated
  • Set a time every day for exercise
  • Find a work out buddy
  • Be patient and consistent
