About Me

My name is Dagny Hedberg. I chose to write a website about health improvement because health is very important to me. In recent years, I started improving my diet, exercising, and doing yoga, and the way my body feels is a lot better because of it. I strongly encourage anyone who visits my page to take care of your body because it will greatly improve your quality of life.

A little more about me: I am a Communication Arts major at UNH Manchester and a teller at St. Mary's Bank in Milford. I enjoy playing the violin, juggling, and listening to music. My favorite band is Between the Buried and Me. My passion is travel and I hope to find a career that allows me to see the world.

I must give credit to my yoga studio, Forever Yoga in Milford, NH, for encouraging me to practice yoga regularly. The owner Jessica is nice, accommodating, and a great teacher. I highly recommend the studio especially to beginners because she has a variety of classes that will fit anyone's needs.
