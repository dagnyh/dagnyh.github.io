Meditation

Turn Your Focus Inward

Meditation reduces stress, clears your mind, and helps you better understand yourself. If you practice a little each day, you will be happier, calmer, and better equipped to deal with the stresses of everyday life.

General Types of Meditation

Focus Difficulty Examples
Focused Attention On single object Medium Buddhist, Chakra Meditation, Pranayama, Sound Meditation, Mantra Meditation
Open Monitoring No specific focus Medium Mindfulness meditation, Vipassana, some kinds of Taoist Meditation
Effortless Presence No focus Hard Self-Enquiry, Dzogchen, Mahamudra, some kinds of Taoist Meditation

Benefits of Meditation

  • Calms you down
  • Relieves stress
  • Slows aging of brain
  • Improves focus
  • Boosts test scores

Tips for Beginners

  • Sit on a comfortable chair or cushion
  • Keep your back straight
  • Set a timer
  • Start by focusing on your breath
  • Gently bring your attention back to your breath when it wanders
