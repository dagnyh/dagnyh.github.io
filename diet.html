Diet

Eat Right, Feel Good

Eating well is extremely important for your health. Learn what to eat, what not to eat, and tips on how to eat healthy.

Foods to Eat

Choose nutrient-dense foods that will give you energy and sustain your body.

  • Fresh fruits
  • Fresh vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Organic foods

Ingredients to Avoid

Deciding what you shouldn't eat is just as important as deciding what you should eat. Limit these foods and additives:

  • Sugar
  • Preservatives
  • Microwave dinners
  • Excessive sodium

Tips for Healthy Eating

Here are a few tips:

  • Control portion size
  • Eat small meals throughout the day rather than 3 large meals
  • Follow up exercise with protein intake
  • Pack lunches and cook at home rather than going out to eat

Don't Support Factory Farming

Factory farming treat animals with horrific cruelty. The animals stay in small cages their entire lives and never see the light of day. In addition, factory farming is extremely damaging to your health, the environment, and family farmers. Buy animal products from local farms rather than the grocery store. Please visit ASPCA for more information.
